<strong>Mob Behaviours<strong>
Makes mobs more smarter

Cave Spiders
Gain permanent Infested effect
Poison players within 1 block when they are within 3 blocks

Creepers
Extremely small chance (5 in 1,000,000 every tick) to summon lightning on themselves, becoming charged

Drowned
Gain Speed II for 2 seconds when in water and a player is within 16 blocks

Endermen
When angry and within 128 blocks of a player’s line of sight, give the player Darkness and display an actionbar warning
Break nearby boats within 2 blocks
Wear a custom head in the Nether and a different custom head in the End (in progress)

Husks
Gains permanent Fire Resistance

Iron Golems
When angry and a player is within 16 blocks, gain Speed II briefly

Phantoms
Give nearby players within 8 blocks Weakness for 5 seconds

Skeletons
Randomly receive enchanted bows (Power I, Power II + Flame, or Power II + Punch)
Some skelly wags gain a shield and can become mounted “Skeleton King” horsemen on skeleton horses
Shield skeletons become silent

Spiders
Very small chance to place cobwebs when near players
Very small chance to summon a cave spider that mounts them

Strays
Chance to freeze water beneath them into frosted ice
Give nearby players Slowness II for 3 seconds
Always receive Power II bows

Witches
Buff nearby hostile mobs within 10 blocks with Strength II, Speed II, and Resistance II
At 1 HP gain Invisibility, Speed III, and Regeneration

Zombies
Small chance to summon reinforcement zombies when hurt
Reinforcements spawn weakened
Small chance to become Miner Zombies with iron pickaxes and torches
Miner Zombies can break cobblestone, deepslate, and netherrack if enabled in settings
